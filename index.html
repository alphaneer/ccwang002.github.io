How to pronounce my nickname?

亮亮 is composed of single character, 亮, repeated two times with same pronunciation, lia-ng. Mind the pitch because in Chinese different pitches point to different words. The pitch is similar to how people say shoot!.

Use Google translator and click on the bottom-right of any panel to hear the pronunciation. It does a fair job. Also, I'd like to recommend an online dictionary, moedict (萌典), for its accurate pronunciation and thorough content. Click and it reads the whole page.

亮 means bright in Chinese.

How to pronounce my name?

I'll assume you've known how to said my nickname, because my full name, 王亮博, should be two-level harder :)

Google translator still does a fair job here. Also, following the link to moedict, click on each character will lead you to their definition pages, where their meaning and pronunciation can be found.

In fact, 王亮博 maps to Wang Liang-Bo respectively. Last name (王) is first stated in Chinese but follows the common English nomenclature when it is written in English.
