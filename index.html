How to pronunce my (nick)name?

亮亮 is composed of a character 亮 repeated two times with same pronuncitaion, liang. Mind the pitch because in Chinese different pitches point to different words. Use Google translator and click on the bottom-right of any panel to hear the pronuncitaion. It does a fair job.

Also, I'd like to recommend an online dictionary moedict (萌典) for its accurate pronuncitaion and thorough content. Click and it reads the whole page.

亮 means bright in Chinese.
